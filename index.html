Our mission

GoCharger designs and manufactures portable charging devices. The company hopes to revolutionize charging technology, with the ultimate goal of using renewable energy.

About Us

We’re a three man team that aims to change how we charge our phones. Our product looks to fix the hassle of bringing a portable charger or a plug-in one with you when you go out. With our GoChargers we’re planning to make a small, environmentally safe and efficient charger that you can share with friends, family and acquaintances.
